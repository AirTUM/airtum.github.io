# AirTUM — airtum.de

This is the source repository for the **[airtum.de](https://airtum.de)** website.

AirTUM is a student community at TUM Campus Heilbronn for people who design, build, and fly fixed-wing aircraft. We are hosted at NXTSpace Heilbronn and open to all students.

---

**You're probably looking for the website: [airtum.de](https://airtum.de)**

---

## About

- Community of plane builders at TUM Campus Heilbronn
- Based at NXTSpace Heilbronn
- First project: autonomous air quality monitoring UAV (CPS module, BIE SS26)
- Everything we build is open source
